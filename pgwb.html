Twang

Best Time To Visit:- November To February

One of the famous beach in Gujurat which is located in the kutch region of Gujurat. It includes lovely Golden sand that provides an excellent contrast to the emerald blue waters sweeping the shores. Lying close to the city's center.You can reach it easily by taking an auto or a cab.Tourist visiting this exquisite beach can enjoy an array of water spotes.They are speed boating, surfting parasailing, paragliding and waterscooting.

Famous For :-

1. Take pleasant camel ride.
2. You can visit the Vijay Vilas Palace located close to third.
